## üß¨ The SOG Framework

ECF Compass implements the **Sovereignty-Obsession-Growth (SOG)** assessment model:

### Three Dimensions

**üõ°Ô∏è Sovereignty (S)**  
Measures autonomy, control, and self-determination in AI interactions. Evaluates the degree to which users maintain agency over their cognitive processes and decisions.

**‚ö†Ô∏è Obsession (O)**  
Assesses dependency patterns, compulsive behaviors, and cognitive capture. Identifies warning signs of unhealthy attachment to AI systems.

**üå± Growth (G)**  
Tracks personal development, skill acquisition, and meaningful progress. Evaluates whether AI interactions contribute to human flourishing.
